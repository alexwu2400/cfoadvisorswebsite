CFO Playbook: Investor Relations
Core element Key principle Success metrics Common mistakes
Narrative control Craft a memorable narrative investors can accurately repeat. Investors convert based on your story. Allowing disconnected executive messaging.
Feedback loops Transform investor talks into strategic intelligence & decisions. Investor questions become more strategic over time. Collecting feedback without acting on insights.
Crisis readiness Take control of potential problems months before they surface. Maintain valuation premium during downturns. Waiting for crises to address known concerns.
Stage-Appropriate IR Build IR capabilities like a sales funnel: target, qualify, convert. Meetings align with next-stage investor profiles. Focusing on the same investor profiles as you mature.
Use of Technology Leverage Apply AI selectively to external analysis & succinct summaries. Proactively anticipate & address investor questions. Sharing excessive metrics that create noise.
